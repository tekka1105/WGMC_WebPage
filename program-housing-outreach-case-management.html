Housing, Healthcare, and Hope (HHH)

The HHH program bridges housing and healthcare for community members experiencing homelessness or housing insecurity.

Call WGMC Patient Information

Overview

The HHH program bridges housing and healthcare for our community members who are experiencing homelessness or are housing insecure. Program participants are provided with services focused on obtaining stable housing and healthcare, including the provision of basic needs, shelter referrals, case management, care coordination, tenant-based rental assistance, and medical respite.

Our team meets people where they are, coordinates healthcare and housing supports, and walks alongside participants through stabilization.

Street Outreach

Connecting with individuals in the community to address urgent needs, offer basic supplies, and link people to healthcare, housing support, and case management.

Tenant-Based Rental Assistance

Helping participants secure and maintain housing through rental assistance, care coordination, and ongoing support to remain stably housed.

Medical Respite

Short-term, home-like stays with coordinated care for individuals who need a safe place to recover while connecting to housing and healthcare resources.

About WGMC

WGMC is a free care organization providing healthcare and social services in Will & Grundy Counties. Free medical and behavioral healthcare is provided to uninsured and underinsured adults, and free housing support and case management is provided to residents who are homeless or housing insecure.
